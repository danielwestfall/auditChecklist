This means that if the website has time limits (like session timeouts), you should be able to turn them off, adjust them, or get more time.
Check for:
  • Time limits: Are there any time limits on the website?
  • Adjustments: Can you turn off the time limit, adjust it, or extend it?
  • Exceptions: Does the time limit fit into any of the exceptions (real-time event, essential, 20 hours)?

This means that if there are things moving or blinking on the website, you should be able to pause them, stop them, or hide them.
Check for:
  • Moving content: Is there any moving, blinking, scrolling, or auto-updating content?
  • Controls: Can you pause, stop, or hide the content?
  • Exceptions: Is the movement essential to an activity?
